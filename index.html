About

Mr Takeout started out as The Grocery Bag in Andover in 1977. The founder was named George Howie and the store has three simple concepts they have followed since it opened its doors; Quality, Service, and speed. Howie's son, Jonathan has expanded the restauraunt's variety with pizza, calzones, and fresh salads
